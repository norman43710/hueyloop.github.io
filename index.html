Where It Began

Our Story

Huey Loop is more than a name. It is a real place.

In 2006, I walked into an old Army hangar on Huey Loop at Simmons Army Airfield and began my journey in military aviation. At the time, I thought I was simply beginning a career. Looking back, I believe God was beginning a much greater story.

Over the years, I served alongside remarkable people. Together we experienced hardship, sacrifice, laughter, friendship and moments that shaped our lives in ways we could not fully appreciate at the time.

Years later, after deployments, leadership opportunities, successes, failures and countless lessons, I found myself standing once again where it all began.

Back on Huey Loop.

Standing there, I realized something that has never left me. The buildings were never what made that place special.

The people did.

The conversations. The friendships. The mentors. The families. The sacrifices. The faith. The memories. The stories.

That realization became the foundation of Huey Loop.

Places may begin our stories, but people give them meaning.

Our Promise

Huey Loop is a Christian, military family-owned business built on faith, integrity and a commitment to preserving stories that matter.

While our family's journey has been deeply influenced by military service, Huey Loop exists to preserve the stories of people from every walk of life.

Our mission is simple: preserve stories, honor lives and help build legacies that endure for generations.
